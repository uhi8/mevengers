Democratizing MEV Defense

Three-Layer Architecture

1. Uniswap V4 Hook

Deployed directly on Unichain. Calculates metrics and detects MEV extraction signatures in real-time execution.

2. Reactive Network

Serves as our autonomous coordinator. Catches off-chain alerts and dispatches immediate 'lock' callbacks.

3. Telegram Governance

Community 'Guardians' receive alerts and bid to establish new, safe fee levels for the compromised pool.

The Defense Lifecycle

1. Detect & Lock

Hook calculates the MEV score. Reactive Sentinel securely locks the pool.

2. Community Alert

Guardians are instantly notified of suspicious activity via the Telegram Bot.

3. Guardian Bidding

Community submits proposed safe fees and protective ETH bids.

4. Auto Settlement

Sentinel unlocks pool, updates the fee, and compensates the original victims.

Core Logic & Smart Contracts

  • MEVengersHook.sol: Uses the _beforeSwap function to calculate MEV scores based on frequency and volume spikes, emitting an MEVAlert.
  • Reactive Sentinel: Handles the autonomous lifecycle, securely firing the settlement callback exactly 3 minutes after a pool lock.
  • IntegrationTest.t.sol: Comprehensive Foundry test suite rigorously verifying the entire flow from detection to insurance distribution.
